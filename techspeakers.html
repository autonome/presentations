Mozilla Tech Speakers

Dietrich Ayala
April 22, 2015

What is Tech Speakers?

  • Community-driven technical speaker development program...
  • Designed to be self-guided or run in your group...
  • In order to increase developer awareness and adoption of Mozilla products and the Open Web.
  • In order to communicate clearly to your teams and groups!

Nothing is free.

  • Being a good speaker is not magic.
  • But becoming one might be uncomfortable.
  • And will take a little bit of work.
  • But a little work goes a LONG way.

It's all about you.

I am not running this.

You are, as a group.

It'll be hard. But fun!

The Program

  • Six weeks short.
  • Combines content crafting and speaking practice.
  • It is repeatable.

Story Crafting

  • Who is your audience?
  • What do you want them to do?
  • What is the bottom line?

The ThoughtBox

The ThoughtBox

  • A tool to craft your story.
  • Organizes your thoughts.
  • Makes sure you cover all the bases.
  • Print as landscape and get to work.

Speaking Practice

Record yourself...

Talking about anything...

For 2 minutes...

Watch as a group.

Repeat!

Feedback Guidelines

  • Watch entire presentation without comment.
  • At the end, go around the group.
  • Say what you liked.
  • Say what you want to see more of.
  • Repeat!

tl;dr Schedule

  • Week 1. Introduction. Homework: Pick a topic.
  • Week 2. Watch recordings! HW: ThoughtBox.
  • Week 3. Recordings + group feedback. HW: Preso outline.
  • Week 4. Recordings + group feedback. HW: Preso draft.
  • Week 5. Recordings + group feedback. HW: Preso polish.
  • Week 6. Final presentations. Party party party.
